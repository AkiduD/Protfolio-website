Why every frontend developer should understand design

April 2026 · 7 min read

You don't need to be a designer to care about design. Understanding spacing rhythm, colour theory, and type hierarchy makes you a dramatically better developer because you start seeing what's wrong before it ships.

The best frontends come from developers who can look at a design and ask “why?” not just “how?”. When you understand the intent behind a layout decision, you can implement it more faithfully, push back with informed opinions, and invent better solutions when constraints change.

I recommend every frontend developer spend at least a month trying to design something from scratch. The struggle teaches you more than any tutorial.
